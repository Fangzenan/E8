Other courses

Certificate II Animal Care

The Certificate II in Animal Care replaces (supersedes) the Certificate II in Animal Studies (ACM20117). The Certificate II in Animal Care is an ideal entry point into the animal care and management industry. This qualification prepares students for entry into sectors of the animal care and management industry, where workers provide care for animals in workplaces such as pet shops, animal shelters, kennels, catteries, groomers, sanctuaries and veterinary clinics.

Certificate III Animal Care Services (Pet Grooming)

The Certificate III Animal Care Services – Pet Grooming, is theNEWAustralian Nationally Recognised qualification for dog groomers. The Certificate III in Animal Care Services – Pet Grooming replaces (supersedes) the Certificate III in PetGrooming (ACM30617). This is the most comprehensive and detailed course available in Australia. This qualification gives you the knowledge, skills and techniques to work as a dog groomer in the pet industry. Students concentrate on the grooming of dogs, including brushing, combing, clipping nails, cleaning ears, bathing, drying techniques, clipping and scissor finishing techniques as well as proper pattern setting for various breeds.

Animal Welfare Advanced Certificate

This comprehensive Animal Welfare certificate is perfect for anyone wanting to learn the skills required to open up opportunities in industries which care about the protection ad wellbeing of domestic or wild animals. This course has been designed by industry professionals to kick start your journey into the world of animals and develop the core knowledge to elevate you to stand out in a very competitive and sought after industry. Gain the confidence and knowledge to begin work or start a business in a field you are passionate about so going to work never feels like a job again!

Certificate in Reptile Conservation

With a specific focus on herpetology, you will develop an ability to understand the biological and ecological characteristics of reptiles and amphibians as well as how to care for them and how to keep them at home. You will also learn how to conduct flora and fauna surveys, learn about wildlife conservation theory, habitat rehabilitation, and threatened species recovery

Wildlife Veterinary Assistant Certificate

This introductory course is designed for anyone who works or cares for animals, from domestic animals at home, to wildlife and farm animals, or in the pet care industry. From veterinary health, disease prevention, animal first aid and rehabilitation care, you will learn broad and varied knowledge and skills, in which you can take with you into any role where you are working or caring for animals. You will also learn about the different ways to conserve wildlife, including the impact habitat use and fragmentation, can have, and about a variety of survey techniques to be used to better understand wildlife, flora, vegetation and marine life.
